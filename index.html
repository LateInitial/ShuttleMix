How rankings work

Points — Your pair's score in each match is added to your total. Win 21–15 and you earn 21 pts; your opponents earn 15 pts each. More points = higher rank.
2

1
League points — If your pair wins the match, each player on that pair earns 3 points. The losing pair earns 0. If the match ends in a draw, every player earns 1 point.
2
Wins — Match wins (not draws) are tracked separately. If league points are tied, more wins ranks higher.

Fewest Games Played — Still tied? The player who reached the same standing in fewer games ranks higher.
